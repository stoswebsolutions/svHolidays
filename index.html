Our Desert Activities

Imagine yourself amidst the desert, the yellow sandstone buildings surrounded by the glorious dunes, and further enhanced by the golden rays of the sun. This is
what a trip to Sam Sand Dunes feels like. This marvellous beauty of Rajasthan is an amazing cultural hub. Stretched across the Thar desert, Sam Sand Dunes is a
prominent part of Jaisalmer.

Indulge in stargazing, stay at the beautiful desert camps, and explore the vivid desert life. With all this and more, the Sam Sand Dunes are one of the most popular
sites for offering a worthwhile experience to its visitors.

Our Desert Camps

Discover Jaisalmer offers an excellent opportunity to live in the desert, just like the nomads of Rajasthan. Our Desert camps usually designed in traditional
campsite decors. Rajasthani cultural performances in the evening usually become the routine schedule atour camp. We Provide the desert safaris which let you explore
the landscape's beauty of the Thar, on a camel's back. We also provide a village safari to visit the nearby villages; a sneak peek into their folklores.

Jaisalmer remains hot all through the year. It experiences very hot summer days from the month of April to the month of July, rainy season from the month of July to the
month of August, and milder winter days from the month of December to the month of March.
